# NOSS — Nosso Open Source Summit

## Sponsorship Proposal

Version 1.0 — April 2026

---

## 1. Overview

NOSS — Nosso Open Source Summit — is a Brazilian event dedicated to **FLOSS (Free/Libre and Open Source Software)**.

The initiative is grounded in a simple reality: open technologies sustain global digital infrastructure, yet the people and communities behind them often operate with limited visibility, weak coordination, and ongoing sustainability challenges.

NOSS acts as a point of articulation for this ecosystem in Brazil, bringing together communities, contributors, and organizations through technical content, shared experiences, and collaboration.

---

## 2. Objectives

* Strengthen the FLOSS ecosystem in Brazil
* Connect communities, companies, and contributors
* Increase visibility of projects and maintainers
* Reduce entry barriers for new contributors
* Support long-term sustainability and continuity

---

## 3. Event structure

* 3 keynotes (60 minutes each)
* Main track: 6 to 8 technical talks (30 min + 5 min Q&A)
* Parallel track — FLOSS Fundamentals:

  * 6 sessions (30 min + 10 min Q&A)
* Final panel

**Total duration:** approximately 9 hours of live streaming

To ensure operational quality and predictability, the event is structured across three execution scenarios:
